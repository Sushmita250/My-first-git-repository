Cockapoo

A cockapoo also known as a cockerpoo is a dog crossbreed bred from the Cocker Spaniel and the Poodle, most commonly the Miniature Poodle.Temperament: Intelligent, Outgoing, Friendly, Active, Loving Height: 36 – 38 cm Mass: 5.4 – 11 kg (Adult) Color: All colors and combinations of colors
