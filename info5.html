Cavalier_King_Charles

The Cavalier King Charles Spaniel is a British breed of toy dog of spaniel type. Four colours are recognised: Blenheim, tricolour, black and tan, and ruby; the coat is smooth and silky. The lifespan is usually between eight and twelve years.
